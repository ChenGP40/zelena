Green Energy? What's that?
Green energy is energy that can be produced using a source that does not harm the natural environment. It is commonly produced using renewable energy sources including solar power, wind power, and hydropower. Using green energy rather than fossil fuels can reduce the amount of greenhouse gas emissions in the atmosphere and slow climate change. Everyone who makes an effort to use green energy solutions contributes to a cleaner environment and a more sustainable future.
